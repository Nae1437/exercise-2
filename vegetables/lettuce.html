Lettuce

My Take:

A vegetable that most commonly is eaten in the form of a salad or sandwich and tends to be light green in appearance.

Lettuce is truly a wonderful blanket for the many ingredients of a salad.
The texture of lettuce improves any salad and its taste is easily determined by any dressing of your choice.
