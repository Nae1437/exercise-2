Broccoli

My Take:

A vegetable that often gets a bad rap, but in all honestly is a tiny and cute tree that you can eat.

While it may not be everyone's favorite vegetable, I do enjoy broccoli, especially in the form of a stirfry.
Something about its resemblance to a tree and how its taste can take on many flavors is a win in my book.
