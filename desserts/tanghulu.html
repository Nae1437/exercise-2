Tanghulu

My Take:

Tanghulu is a dessert that features dessert and candied sugar.
Some of the most popular kinds of tanghulu are strawberry, grape, and so on.

Whether you consider tanghulu to be more fruit than dessert or more dessert than fruit, many can agree that it is very satisfying and delicious.
My absolute favorite kind of tanghulu is with candied strawberries as it is perfectly sweet and crunchy.
