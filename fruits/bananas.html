Bananas

My Take:

A fruit that arguably is easier than most others to incorporate into a wide variety of recipes like smoothies, pancakes, ice cream among many more healthier desserts, or simply on it's own.

One fruit that despite being fun to peel/open, I do not like in any form or receipe to date.
The texture and taste are a hard pass, though the smell can be quite nice.
